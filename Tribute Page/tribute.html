Rameshbabu Praggnanandhaa

The chess grandmaster

Biograpghy

Rameshbabu Praggnanandhaa is an Indian chess player. He became the fourth-youngest person ever to achieve the title of Grandmaster. He also became the youngest international Master in the history of chess at the age of 10 years. Praggnanandhaa started playing chess at a very early age and won the world youth chess championship at the age of seven.

Key Details

  • Born: 10 August 2005
  • Country & State: Chennai, Tamil Nadu
  • Achievements: Chess Olympiad (2022),World Youth Chess Championships,FIDE World Cup (2023),Candidates Tournament Qualification (2024)
  • Title:Grandmaster
